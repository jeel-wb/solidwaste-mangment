Understanding Waste

Proper sorting is the first step towards a cleaner planet.

Organic Waste

Biodegradable materials like food scraps and yard waste. Converts into nutrient-rich compost.

Recyclable

Materials like paper, glass, and clean plastics that can be processed into new products.

Hazardous

Toxic, flammable, or corrosive waste. Includes batteries, chemicals, and medical waste.

General Waste

Non-recyclable, non-hazardous waste destined for landfills. We must minimize this!
